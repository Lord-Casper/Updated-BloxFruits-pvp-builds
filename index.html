1. E-Claw Rumble Yama Kabucha

This build is quite versatile with great potential in both air and ground-based PvP. Adding cyborg makes it overpowered. The stats you need are Melee, Defense, and Sword. There are many one-shot combos with this build, making it good for dealing with teamers, runners, and air campers.

2. Superhuman Rumble Soul Cane Acidum Rifle

This build works well with cyborg, and is made specifically for 1v1s, making it perfect for bounty hunting when there is only one opponent. It excels in both land and air combat. The Acidum Rifle moves are easy to hit, making the combos easy to execute. You can obtain this build in the 2nd sea, and it can fence a 30 million bounty quite fairly.

3. Superhuman/Godhuman Gravity Kabucha Gravity Cane

This build is similar to the ice build but requires more skill and can be more effective if performed correctly. Its effectiveness depends on the user’s skill, making it either a great threat or nothing to worry about.

4. Superhuman/Godhuman Spirit Spikey Trident Soul Guitar/Kabucha

This build depends heavily on the user's experience due to its extensive list of combos that are effective but difficult to land. With enough skill, you can become a true "Raging Demon."

5. E-Claw Rumble Bisento-v2/Spikey Trident Kabucha

This build is special because it is both easy to hit and deals significant damage. It can one-shot a 30 million bounty, as per my experience. This build is underrated by many but has helped many players reach 30 million bounty with ease.
